## App Description

### Full Description

Psoriatlas is the comprehensive reference guide for FDA-approved psoriasis treatments, designed specifically for patients and healthcare professionals seeking detailed, accurate information about available therapies.

**Key Features:**

📱 **Complete FDA Drug Database** - Browse 40+ approved treatments including biologics, biosimilars, and conventional therapies

🔍 **Powerful Search & Filtering** - Find treatments by name, manufacturer, drug type, or therapeutic class

💊 **Detailed Drug Information** Including:
- FDA approval dates
- Administration methods (injections, oral, topical, IV)
- Dosing guidelines
- Known side effects
- Approved age groups
- Manufacturer information
- Biological targets for immunotherapy drugs

🏷️ **Organized by Drug Class**
- TNF-alpha inhibitors
- IL-17 inhibitors
- IL-23 inhibitors
- JAK inhibitors
- Phosphodiesterase-4 inhibitors
- Systemic and topical corticosteroids
- Vitamin D analogues
- And more

⭐ **Favorites System** - Bookmark important drugs for quick reference

📲 **Completely Offline** - All data stored locally on your device, no internet required for reference

🔒 **Privacy First** - No personal data collection or tracking

**Drug Categories Included:**
- Biologics and biosimilars from major manufacturers
- Small molecule treatments
- Topical therapies
- Oral systemic treatments
- Generic alternatives

Perfect for:
- Newly diagnosed patients exploring treatment options
- Dermatologists researching latest therapies
- Healthcare providers evaluating medications
- Pharmacists and nurses managing patient care
- Anyone seeking accurate, organized psoriasis treatment information

**Disclaimer:** This app is for informational purposes only. Always consult with a qualified dermatologist or healthcare provider before starting any treatment. The information provided is accurate as of publication but may be subject to change.

## Support Content

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my personal health data stored in this app?**
A: No. All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal health information.

**Q: How often is the drug database updated?**
A: We update the database with new FDA approvals and changes. Check the app for update availability in the App Store.

**Q: Can I use this instead of talking to my doctor?**
A: No. This app is for informational reference only. Always consult with a qualified dermatologist or healthcare provider before starting any new treatment.

**Q: Do I need internet to use this app?**
A: No. The entire drug database is stored locally on your device and works completely offline.

**Q: Is this app updated with new drugs?**
A: Yes, we regularly update the database with new FDA approvals and modifications to existing treatments.

**Q: Can I export or share the drug information?**
A: Currently, the app is for personal reference. Future versions may include export features.
